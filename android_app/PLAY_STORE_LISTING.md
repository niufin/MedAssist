## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**MedAssist** is an intelligent, all-in-one healthcare management platform designed to streamline medical operations for Doctors, Pharmacists, Lab Technicians, and Patients. Built with compliance to Indian healthcare regulations, MedAssist ensures secure, efficient, and modern medical care delivery.

### Key Features:

**For Doctors:**
*   **AI-Powered Diagnosis:** Get intelligent assistance with diagnosis suggestions.
*   **Instant Prescriptions:** Generate and manage digital prescriptions effortlessly.
*   **Patient History:** Access comprehensive patient medical records and history in one place.
*   **Consultation Management:** Handle patient consultations efficiently with a dedicated dashboard.

**For Pharmacists:**
*   **Digital Fulfillment:** Process digital prescriptions instantly.
*   **Inventory Tracking:** Manage medicine stocks and inventory in real-time.
*   **Doctor Collaboration:** Seamlessly connect with prescribing doctors for clarifications.

**For Lab Technicians:**
*   **Report Management:** Direct upload of lab reports.
*   **OCR Integration:** Smart text analysis for test reports.
*   **Test Tracking:** Streamlined management of patient diagnostic tests.

**For Patients:**
*   **Secure Consultations:** Connect with doctors for online or in-person consultations.
*   **Medical Records:** Keep track of your prescriptions, lab reports, and health history.
*   **Privacy First:** Your data is protected with strict adherence to Indian privacy laws.

### App Highlights:
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Clean, intuitive design for all users.
*   **Smart Navigation:** Unified dashboard with easy access to all features.
*   **Reliable Performance:** Optimized with native error handling and smart refresh controls.
*   **Secure & Compliant:** Adheres to the Information Technology Act, 2000, SPDI Rules 2011, and Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020.
